About Us

Come and enjoy the music and sites of Tzaneen. The main focus of this site is to enjoy the Nature of Limpopo and the sites that Limpopo has to offer. We also want to play the music that the people want to hear. Music that brings back good times, good memories and good feelings while listening to the music on the site. We will have a group that you can put requests on and the song will be added if the song is public friendly.
